Premise

Rant 01 exists so the shorter side of the site has something to hold onto. The tone is meant to be a little sharper than the posts, even if the content itself remains mostly harmless placeholder material.

That tonal shift is useful because it lets us test whether category pages can feel distinct without demanding a separate visual system.

Exit Line

If a short complaint page reads cleanly and looks deliberate, then the underlying template is probably flexible enough to support both essays and quicker, punchier entries.
